Butter Chicken

Chicken marinade ingredients:

  • 800g of chicken thighs or breasts cut in small pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Sauce ingredients:

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp ghee
  • 1 large, chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek

Instructions:

  1. Add chicken to marinade ingredients and allow to sit for at least one hour, ideally overnight if possible
  2. Preheat oil in pan and add chicken in batches until browned on all sides
  3. Heat ghee in same pan and add onions once hot. Scrape fond off the bottom of the pan and incorporate with the onions
  4. Add garlic and ginger, allowing 1 minute to cook until fragrant. Follow up with dry spices for an additional 20 seconds
  5. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for about 10-15 minutes, salt to taste
  6. Remove mixture from heat and add to a blender in batches, alternatively use an immersion blender. Blend until mixture is very smooth. Add water in small amounts as needed
  7. Pour smoothed sauce back in the pan and add in the cream, sugar, and fenugreek. Bring to simmmer and add cooked chicken to the sauce for about 8-10 minutes
  8. Serve over rice or alongside naan bread
