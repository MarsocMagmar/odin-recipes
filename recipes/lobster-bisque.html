Lobster Bisque

Ingredients:

  • 4 lobster tails
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 peeled and chopped carrots
  • 2 stalks of chopped celery
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp chopped tarragon
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 1 1/4 cup sherry
  • 4 cups lobster stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Fill a large pot with about 5 cups of water and lightly salt. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add lobster tails to the boiling water and cook until tails are a deep red color
  3. Remove cooked tails from the boiling water and extract meat from the shells, set aside
  4. Add shells back into the water, reduce heat and allow to reduce until 4 cups of lobster stock can be obtained
  5. Heat butter and oil in a sauce pan, add onions and cook over low heat until onions are browned and soft. Add garlic and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes
  6. Add carrot and celery to the pot and allow to cook for 1-2 minutes, they will finish cooking through when the soup is simmering
  7. Ad bouillon powder, salt and pepper to the vegetables and cook an additional 1-2 minutes
  8. Reduce heat and add tomato paste and flour, ensuring that vegetables are completely covered
  9. Add sherry and allow for alcohol to cook off, follow this up with the lobster stock and allow to come up to a light simmer
  10. Once carrots and celery have become soft, transfer mixture into a blender and blend in batches until smooth
  11. Return soup to the pan and add in cream
  12. Set aside some cooked lobster to use as a garnish, add the remainder to the soup
  13. Serve in a bowl alongside bread and drizzled in some heavy cream
