Nashville Hot Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 560g flour
  • 3-7 Tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 3/4 Tbsp salt
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp black pepper
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 hotsauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 750mL frying oil
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1-2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Add flour, 1 Tbsp cayenne pepper, 1 Tbsp paprika, 1 Tbsp salt, 1 Tbsp pepper to a bowl and mix thoroughly
  2. Add eggs, hotsauce and buttermilk to a seperate bowl and mix until eggs are beaten
  3. Dip pieces of chicken in the flour mixture, into the egg mixture and then back into the flour mixture one piece at a time. Set chicken aside once coated
  4. Heat oil to 350 degrees Fahrenheit, add chicken into the oil in batches and fry until lightly golden brown on both sides
  5. Allow oil to cool for several minutes before adding 1/2 cup of the oil to the butter. Addin the rest of the dry spices to the butter and oil mixture. Add more oil if needed to make sure that all ingredients are incorporated.
  6. Coat chicken in the spice mixture and serve over white bread with pickles
